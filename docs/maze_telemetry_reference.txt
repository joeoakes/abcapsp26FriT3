Maze mission summaries describe a robot run through a maze. A mission summary may include robot_id, mission_type, start_time, end_time, moves_left_turn, moves_right_turn, moves_straight, moves_reverse, moves_total, distance_traveled, duration_seconds, mission_result, and abort_reason.

moves_left_turn counts successful left-turn moves.
moves_right_turn counts successful right-turn moves.
moves_straight counts successful forward moves.
moves_reverse counts successful backward moves.
moves_total is the total number of successful moves.
distance_traveled is the total path distance traveled during the mission.
duration_seconds is the total time spent in the mission.
mission_result is typically success or aborted.
abort_reason explains why the mission ended when it was not successful.
